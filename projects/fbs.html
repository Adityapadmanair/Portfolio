About

What began as a fun little solo project is now a fully fledged app. I’ve been a big fan of football and coding and I thought why not mash it up together? Presenting to you the Football Betting Simulator. Play the betting game without real money and have fun. The odds are all made by a particular algorithm that I have curated. I have more exciting plans for the app, like adding more leagues, scoreboards, battling other users and more. This is just a beginning. Stay tuned for more.

How it Works

  • Users start with in-game currency.
  • Place bets on fixtures with odds-based rewards.
  • Integrated league tables help in predictions.
  • The bets open when it is 3 days before the matchday.
  • If the user predicts correctly, the user will get the amount of in game currency times the odds.
  • The results are updated once all the games for the matchweek are done.
